Chocoloate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 10 gal of milk
  • 5 eggs
  • 2 lbs of chocolate chips
  • 1 lb of flour
  • 3 lbs of butter

Method:

  1. Crack 5 eggs and put them in a big bowl
  2. Pour 5 gallons of milk into bowl and stir
  3. Melt 3 pounds of butter and add the pound of flour in a seperate bowl
  4. Pour contents of each bowl into a bigger bowl and manually stir for 3 hours
  5. Add all the choclate chips
  6. Pour contents into multiple pans and bake at 350 degrees Fahrenheit
